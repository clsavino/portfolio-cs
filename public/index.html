Three Words That Describe Me

Cooking - CODING - Counseling


Three things I enjoy doing are Coding, Counseling and Cooking. What do they have in common? Puzzle solving! All three start with a problem that needs to be solved just like a puzzle. In each of these endeavors I get to solve the problem by putting together all the pieces.

Currently I am a Full Stack Web Developer honing new skills. My analytical side loves the challenge of breaking a project down into pieces and putting all those pieces together to develop a full stack app.

As a Software Engineer I have worked on fascinating projects. My favorite was software test for the F-16 which included an opportunity to fly in the fight simulator. I have a BS in Computer Science and a Masters degree in Biblical Counseling. I enjoy volunteering as a counselor at church and mentoring other women.

When I'm not coding I love having fun with family and friends playing cards, games or puzzles. Cooking good food that pleases the people I'm with is my other passion.
